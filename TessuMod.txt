JHakonen's mod for integrating TeamSpeak into World of Tanks.

Installation:
 - extract the archive to your world of Tanks folder, e.g: E:\Games\World_of_Tanks
 - if you have other mods installed, it is recommended not to replace CameraNode.pyc or __init__.pyc files

For more information, see:
   http://forum.worldoftanks.eu/index.php?/topic/433614-093-tessumod-wotteamspeak-integration-mod/
